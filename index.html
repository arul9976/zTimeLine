The story of how we got started on Webflow
"Unveiling the journey of excellence: The Zoho timeline showcases memorable product launches and essential milestones."
1996
AdventNet, Inc.
Founders: Sridhar Vembu and Tony Thomas
Initial Focus: Network management and IT infrastructure solutions
2000
Rebranding: AdventNet rebranded to Zoho Corporation.
Product Launch: Zoho Creator, an online database tool for creating custom applications without needing extensive programming knowledge.
2005
Zoho CRM Introduction
Product Launch: Zoho CRM was introduced, offering customer relationship management solutions tailored for businesses of various sizes. Impact: Established Zoho’s presence in the CRM market and provided a foundation for their growing suite of business applications.
2007
Expansion into Cloud Computing
Product Launch: Zoho Office Suite, which included Zoho Writer (word processing), Zoho Sheet (spreadsheets), and Zoho Show (presentations). Significance: Marked Zoho’s entry into the cloud-based productivity tools market, competing with established players like Microsoft Office and Google Workspace.
2010
Zoho Projects and Marketplace
Product Launch: Zoho Projects, a project management tool designed to help teams plan, track, and collaborate on projects.
Marketplace Launch: Zoho Marketplace, an online platform for users to discover and integrate various applications with Zoho products.
2014
Expansion into Cloud Computing
Product Launch: Zoho Desk (customer support and helpdesk software), further expanding their suite of business tools. Zoho Social (social media management).
2015
Data Centers Expansion
Global Expansion: Opened new data centers in the United States, Europe, and Asia to improve service reliability, data sovereignty, and compliance with local regulations.
2016
Zoho Cliq
Product Launch: Zoho Cliq was officially launched in 2016. Purpose: Zoho Cliq is a team communication and collaboration tool designed to facilitate real-time messaging, file sharing, and team collaboration. It includes features such as direct messaging, group chats, voice and video calls, and integration with other Zoho and third-party applications.
2022
Zoho WorkDrive
Product Launch: Zoho WorkDrive, a cloud storage and file management solution designed to enhance team collaboration and document management. Features: Included advanced features for team collaboration, secure file sharing, and file organization.
2024
Advancements in AI and Integration
AI Integration: Focused on integrating artificial intelligence and machine learning into their product suite to enhance automation, predictive analytics, and user experience. Innovations: Introduced new features and improvements across their applications to leverage the latest advancements in AI technology.
